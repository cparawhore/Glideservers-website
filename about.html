What, Why, Where?

It's as easy as gliding!

What are we? Glide Servers is a new business that sells Virtual Private Servers (VPS) to our customers with he addition of bringing great customer service and the best possible experience. We provide a range of different VPS services which include the low end selection as well as the more expensive premium services.

Where are we? We choose to sell our services in Montreal, Canada. This is because it offers a great connection to American customers yet still provides a decent connection quality to those in Europe.

Why choose us? We make outstanding customer service the main priority at Glide Servers. This is because we care about our customers, and wish to give them the best possible experience, whether this entails technical issues, customer support or just a query. We care about you.
